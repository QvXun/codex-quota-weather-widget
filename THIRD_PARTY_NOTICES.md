Weather data is provided by [Open-Meteo](https://open-meteo.com/) under the [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International license](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

When weather is enabled, the widget sends the user-confirmed administrative-area center coordinates to Open-Meteo. Normal network communication also exposes the connection IP to that service. The widget does not send Codex credentials, quota data, session content, or chat content.
